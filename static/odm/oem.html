Who We Are

Kaadas, which focuses on electronic & smart locks, is a leading smart lock enterprise in Asia and a professional smart lock manufacturer with a fully integrated in-house supply chain that can provide complete smart lock solutions for lock companies and integrator partners.

What We Do

Industry Design

Own a strong R&D team to constantly design or customize smart door locks for lock companies and integrator partners. Many products got global industrial design awards such as iF and Reddot.

Structure Engineering

In accordance with industry standards to ensure safety, maintainability and availability. Be able to withstand thousands of strength tests, environment tests, reliability tests, etc.

Hardware Engineering

Supply with component hardware such as latches, cylinders, handles, etc. Stand out among hardware suppliers due to our commitment to quality, considerate service to customers and expertise in custom part design and manufacturing.

Firmware Engineering

Have rich experience, skills and tools to build robust and maintainable systems as well as to do the engineering analysis required to make sure devices function correctly under all conditions.

App & Cloud Development

Provide connected products and solutions with App & Cloud service using cutting-edge technologies to manage and monitor your lock from anywhere and anytime.

Integration

Seamlessly integrate the smart lock with various of smart home systems and security systems.

Manufacturing

Three manufacturing bases with leading automatic assembly lines, CNC machining equipment, die-casting machines and plating lines. Manufacturing capacity is over 5 million sets annually.
